Our Journey

Since our inception in 1998, Meditech Mfg has evolved from a specialized fabrication workshop into a global powerhouse in medical device manufacturing. Our history is defined by a relentless pursuit of engineering excellence, starting with the development of patented surgical alloys that revolutionized the durability of orthopedic instruments. Over the decades, we have expanded our capabilities to include robotic-assisted manufacturing and bio-intelligent monitoring systems, always keeping clinical reliability at the core of our operations.

From humble beginnings to global leadership, our commitment to precision remains unchanged.
We continue to innovate, ensuring that the next generation of medical tools is safer and more effective than ever.

Our Vision

Our vision is to define the future of surgical precision by integrating artificial intelligence with high-fidelity manufacturing. We aim to empower surgeons with tools that act as a natural extension of their expertise, ultimately achieving a world where medical technology eliminates the barriers to successful patient outcomes.

Our Mission

Our mission is to engineer safety and trust into every medical device we produce. By adhering to the highest global quality standards, we provide healthcare professionals with the certainty they need in life-critical situations, ensuring that high-quality medical technology is accessible and reliable across all healthcare tiers.

World-Class Facilities

Our manufacturing facilities are built on the principles of lean healthcare production and environmental sustainability. Each plant features advanced air filtration systems and climate-controlled assembly lines to maintain the absolute sterility required for class III medical devices. We leverage digital twin technology to optimize our manufacturing processes, ensuring that every batch meets the exact specifications required by international health authorities.

Commitment to Excellence

Compliance is the heartbeat of our company. We operate under a rigorous Quality Management System (QMS) that is fully compliant with ISO 13485:2016 and FDA 21 CFR Part 820. This commitment ensures that our products are not only effective but also consistently safe for the patients who depend on them.

ISO 13485:2016

Our Quality Management System exceeds the global standards for medical devices, ensuring every product component is tracked, tested, and validated for clinical reliability in surgeries.

Biocompatible Materials

We utilize medical-grade titanium and proprietary alloys that have undergone rigorous toxicity and longevity testing, ensuring safe long-term human tissue integration without rejection.

FDA & MDR Compliance

Strict adherence to pre-market approval processes and post-market surveillance ensure that our devices meet the latest safety requirements defined by the FDA and EU MDR directives.
