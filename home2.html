Quality Assurance & Testing

Rigorous testing is embedded at every stage of our manufacturing lifecycle. We believe that quality is the primary driver of patient safety, which is why every device undergoes extensive verification before being approved for clinical use.

Automated Inspection

Our AI-driven optical systems perform micron-level measurements on every device, ensuring 100% adherence to design specifications and eliminating the risk of human error in quality control.

Sterility Validation

We utilize multi-stage sterilization protocols, validated to a Sterility Assurance Level (SAL) of 10⁻⁶. This process is critical for preventing hospital-acquired infections (HAIs) and ensuring patient safety.

Stress Testing

Before any product is released, it undergoes accelerated aging and rigorous fatigue testing to simulate years of clinical use, ensuring that our instruments remain reliable in life-critical situations.
