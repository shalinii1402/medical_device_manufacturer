The Verification Lifecycle

Every single device that leaves our facility undergoes a rigorous three-stage verification process.

1

Material Validation

We perform spectroscopy and tensile testing on 100% of raw materials. Only clinical-grade alloys and biocompatible polymers enter our production line.

2

In-Process Inspection

Automated optical systems and robotic calipers monitor production every 0.5 seconds. Any deviation outside of micron-level tolerances triggers an immediate halt.

3

Sterility Assurance

Final products are sterilized and biologically sampled. We hold products in quarantine until independent sterility confirmation is received.
